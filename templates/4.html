For Students

Some students feel stuck if they are not able to get admission to their desired college or institution. Some others are not are not able to decide which stream would be best for them. We support such students by various methods like Personality Tests, Multiple Intelligences Tests, IQ and EQ Tests and by talking to them to know more about their interests and aspirations. It is about finding what motivates a particular student and what will be best, keeping in mind his or her interest and temperament.
